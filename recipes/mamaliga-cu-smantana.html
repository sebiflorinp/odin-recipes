Mamaliga cu smantana

Mamaliga cu smantana a well known traditional Romanian recipe that is beloved by many. It is a bit difficult to cook at first but once you've cooked it for the first time you'll not have any issues cooking it again

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Yellow flour
  • Salt
  • Cheese
  • Creme

Steps

  1. Put water in a pot
  2. Put the pot on a gas cooker
  3. As the water boils pour flour and keep mixing
  4. After you poured all the flour let it boil for 15 more minutes
  5. Put it on a plate
  6. Mix cream with cheese
  7. Put the mix on the same plate
  8. Bon Appetit
