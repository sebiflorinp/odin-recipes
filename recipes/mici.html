Mici

Mici is a Romanian recipe (or at the very least an extremely popular one) which is usually really easy to cook , making it a great recipe for someone who is new to cooking.

Not only is it easy to cook but it also doesn't need many ingredients On top of that it is really tasty and can be easily found in Romanian restaurants.

Ingredients

  • Mici
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Prepare all of the ingredients you need and a pan
  2. Heat the pan
  3. After the pan has heated put the Mici in it
  4. Put salt
  5. Roll them so they are cooked on all sides
  6. Bon Appetit
