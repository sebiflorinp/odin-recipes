Sarmale

Sarmale (known as cabbage rolls anywhere else) is a recipe that is really popular in Romania but also quite difficult cook for a beginner.

The cooking process is long and painful. Additionally, it requires many ingredients. However, it is worth going through the process because you can cook a lot of servings by preparing multiple servings in one go

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Cabbage
  • Onion
  • Pepper
  • Oil
  • Thyme
  • Tomato paste

Steps

  1. Takes the leaves of the cabbage
  2. Cut the leaves in the shapes of a rectangle
  3. Cut the Onion
  4. Heat the pan
  5. Put oil and then cut onion
  6. Put the rice and a bit of water
  7. After a couple of minutes put the mix of rice and onion in a bowl
  8. Put the tomato paste and the thyme
  9. Take a bit of the mix and make a roll with the cut cabbage leaf
  10. Put the rolls in a bowl
  11. Put water
  12. Put the bowl in the oven and wait for an hour
  13. Bon Appetit
